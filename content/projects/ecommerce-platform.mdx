## Project Summary

A comprehensive e-commerce platform that handles everything from product catalog management to payment processing and order fulfillment.

## Key Features

### For Customers
- **Advanced Search**: AI-powered product search with filters
- **Wishlist & Favorites**: Save items for later purchase
- **Multiple Payment Methods**: Stripe, PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay
- **Real-time Inventory**: Live stock updates and availability
- **Customer Reviews**: Rating and review system

### For Vendors
- **Multi-vendor Support**: Multiple sellers on one platform
- **Inventory Management**: Real-time stock tracking and alerts
- **Analytics Dashboard**: Sales reports and customer insights
- **Order Management**: Automated order processing and fulfillment

### For Administrators
- **Admin Panel**: Comprehensive dashboard for platform management
- **User Management**: Role-based access control
- **Analytics & Reporting**: Detailed business intelligence
- **Content Management**: Easy product and content updates

Built with modern web technologies focusing on performance, scalability, and security.
